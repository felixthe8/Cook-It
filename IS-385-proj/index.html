Ready to Cook It?

Cook It! was founded in 2018 on one principle - getting people back into cooking. It is this principle that has driven all the features and services that Cook It! offers. Cook It! lets you pick the meal that you to cook want, without you having to go to the store yourself to pick up the ingredients. Instead, Cook It! coordinates with the store for you. For each meal you order, we will also show you the ingredients and nutrition facts because we truly believe that the best version of you is the one that knows what's going into your body. Cook It! is perfect for all occassions - a romantic night in, a quick meet-up with an old friend, and a fantastic meal that hits the spot after a long day. Create an account, download the Cook It! app, and get started today.

Meals of All Types

Browse our wide array of meals, and pick the ones that you want. Cook It! lets you filter meal selection to your heart's content. Want something sweet? Try the dessert section. Need a pick-me-up before a long day? We have many breakfast meals you'll enjoy.

Detailed Recipes

Every meal you order on Cook It! comes with detailed recipes on how to prepare it. Gone are the days where you have to hope that you put the right amount of everything. Cook It! recipes turn novice chefs into great cooks, all you have to do is follow the instructions.

Straight From Your Store

Each meal that you pick up comes from the local grocery store that you pick. Cook It! customers don't have to wait on a delivery or hope that a store has all the ingredients they need, because every order is handled by the store, and they'll get it right every time!
